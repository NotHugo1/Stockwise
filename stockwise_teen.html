📖 Term of the Day
Market Capitalization
The total value of a company's shares. Calculated by: share price × total shares. Apple is worth ~$3 trillion. That's their "market cap."
Sector Rotation
When investors move money from one industry to another based on economic changes. Like moving from airlines to oil stocks when gas prices spike.
Volatility
How much a stock's price swings up and down. High volatility = risky but potentially big gains or losses.
